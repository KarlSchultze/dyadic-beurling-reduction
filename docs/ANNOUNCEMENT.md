# Public announcement draft

I have released a preliminary research note:

**A Dyadic-to-Integer Beurling Reduction for a Reciprocal Nyman--Beurling Refinement**

This is not a proof of the Riemann Hypothesis. It is a structural reduction showing that a dyadic reciprocal Nyman--Beurling refinement leads to the integer Beurling / Nyman--Baez-Duarte approximation endpoint.

The note includes a projection dichotomy: either the dyadic residual is already controlled by the integer Beurling distance, or the refinement by reciprocal atoms gives a quantitative no-stalling step.

I am sharing this for review and feedback from anyone interested in Nyman--Beurling, Baez-Duarte, or Hilbert-space criteria for RH.
